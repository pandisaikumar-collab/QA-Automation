> Locators (finding elements):

  - Find single element:
    Selenium - driver.find_element(By.ID, "id")
    Playwright - page.locator("#id")

  - Find multiple elements:
    Selenium - driver.find_elements(By.XPATH, "//div[@class='class']")
    Playwright - page.query_selector_all("xpath=//div[@class='class']")

  - By ID:
    Selenium - driver.find_element(By.ID, "id")
    Playwright - page.locator("#id")

  - By Name:
    Selenium - driver.find_element(By.NAME, "name")
    Playwright - page.locator("[name='name']")

  - By Class Name:
    Selenium - driver.find_element(By.CLASS_NAME, "class")
    Playwright - page.locator(".class")
