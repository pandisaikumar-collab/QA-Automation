q) What do mean by the term in Networking:

> Networking is the process of interconnected, devices
   that communicate and share resources with each other.

> these devices can be computers, servers, routers, switches,
  firewalls, and other hardware components.

> Networking enables data exchange, resource sharing,
  and communication across local and wide area networks (LANs and WANs).

> It involves protocols, technologies, and infrastructure
  that facilitate the transfer of data packets between devices.

> Networking is essential for internet connectivity,
  cloud computing, online services, and modern communication systems.

Example:
========
- A home Wi-Fi network connects multiple devices to the internet.
- A corporate network allows employees to access shared files and applications.
- The internet itself is a vast global network connecting millions of devices worldwide.
