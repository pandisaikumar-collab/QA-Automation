q) How many broadcast domains are there in a switch:

 > By default, a switch has one broadcast domain,
 > meaning all devices connected to the switch can receive broadcast
   messages from each other.
 > However, if VLANs (Virtual LANs) are configured on the switch,
   each VLAN creates a separate broadcast domain.
 > Therefore, the number of broadcast domains in a switch depends on
   the number of VLANs configured.

 Example:
    - A switch with no VLANs has 1 broadcast domain.
    - A switch with 3 VLANs configured has 3 broadcast domains.
    - A switch with 5 VLANs configured has 5 broadcast domains.
