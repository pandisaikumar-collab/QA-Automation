Broadcast:
 - Sending to all devices on the network
 - Uses a special broadcast address (e.g., FF:FF:FF:FF:FF:FF for MAC addresses)
 - Common in Layer 2 (Data Link Layer) of the OSI model
 - Examples: ARP requests, DHCP discovery messages
 - Can lead to network congestion if overused
 - Typically limited to the local network segment (does not cross routers)
