1. A device or app that requests services from a server.
2. Examples include web browsers, email clients, and FTP clients.
3. Operates at the Application Layer (Layer 7) of the OSI model.
4. Responsible for initiating communication and presenting data to the user.
5. Communicates with servers to access resources and services over a network.
6. Uses protocols such as HTTP, FTP, SMTP, and DNS to interact with servers.

Example: Browser (like Chrome or Firefox) requesting a webpage from a web server.
