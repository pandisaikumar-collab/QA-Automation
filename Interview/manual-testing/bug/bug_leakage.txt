Bug Leakage:
 - Bug found by customers after the product is released to production.

Example:
  - A customer reports that the checkout process on an e-commerce website is not working.
  - The bug was not identified during the testing phase before the release.
  - The development team investigates and finds that a recent code change caused the issue.
  - The bug is fixed and a patch is released to address the problem.
  - The testing team reviews their test cases to ensure similar issues are caught in future releases.
